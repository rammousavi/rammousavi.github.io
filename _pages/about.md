I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at IDSIA, USI-SUPSI in Lugano, in the group of Fabrizio Grandoni since May 2024. In September 2023, I received my PhD from the University of Alberta under the supervision of Zac Friggstad, and previously earned my Master’s degree from the University of Waterloo.

I am interested in combinatorial optimization with a focus on designing approximation algorithms for NP-hard problems.
